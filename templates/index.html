Printing tips

  • Print the framed board or pieces page on thick cardstock.
  • Cut along the black lines with scissors or a craft knife.
  • Use the reference sheet as the answer key.
  • For little ones, start with Easy (2×2) and a thick Grid.
  • Tick only one page to export a single printable sheet.
